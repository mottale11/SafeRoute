SafeRoute is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our safety-alert and risk-mapping platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to keep the platform useful and safe:

A. Information you provide

  • Account details such as your username and email address.
  • Safety reports you submit, including descriptions, images of risk zones, and approximate location.
  • Feedback, comments, or messages sent to our support team.

B. Automatically collected information

  • Device type, browser type, and usage statistics.
  • Approximate location (only with your permission) to show local risk alerts and heatmaps.

We do not collect sensitive personal information or real-time exact GPS data without your explicit permission.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used to:

  • Display community-generated safety alerts.
  • Improve the accuracy of the safety heatmap.
  • Prevent misuse, false reporting, and abusive activity.
  • Improve platform performance and user experience.

We never sell your data.

3. Sharing of Information

We may share information only:

  • With law-enforcement authorities if required by law.
  • With mapping or analytics services (anonymized only).

Your identity is never shown to other users unless you choose to make it public.

4. Data Security

We use encryption, secure servers, and strict access controls.

However, no system is 100% secure, so we encourage users to share only what is necessary.

5. Your Rights

You may:

  • Edit or delete your account.
  • Remove submitted posts.
  • Request to delete your stored data.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will notify users via email or in-app alerts.

7. Contact

For questions or privacy concerns, contact:

support@saferoute.com
