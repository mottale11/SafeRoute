About SafeRoute Kenya

SafeRoute is a community-powered platform designed to improve personal safety and awareness in cities and towns in Kenya.

Every day, women face risks such as unsafe roads, insecure shortcuts, suspicious activity, harassment hotspots, poorly lit zones, and general insecurity. Yet most of this information is never reported or is shared too late.

Our mission is simple: give women the power to see danger before they walk into it.

How It Works

Using crowdsourced reports from everyday users, SafeRoute Kenya generates a live Safety Heatmap that highlights risk zones, incident-prone areas, and recent alerts. Users can post pictures of unsafe locations, suspicious activity, or areas needing improvement (such as unsafe pedestrian paths, blackspots, etc.). The platform organizes this information into clear visual markers that help others make safer decisions.
