Safety Tips & Guides

Road Safety

  • Always use designated pedestrian crossings and wait for traffic signals
  • Be aware of your surroundings, especially at night
  • Avoid using headphones while walking near roads
  • Make eye contact with drivers before crossing
  • Use well-lit routes and avoid isolated areas
  • Keep your phone charged and accessible for emergencies

Gender-Based Violence Protection

  • Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, it probably is
  • Share your location with trusted contacts when traveling
  • Learn self-defense techniques and carry a personal alarm if legal
  • Avoid sharing too much personal information with strangers
  • Keep emergency contacts easily accessible
  • Report incidents immediately to authorities and on SafeRoute
  • Travel in groups when possible, especially at night
  • Be cautious of accepting rides from strangers

Digital Scams Protection

  • Never share your passwords or PINs with anyone
  • Be cautious of unsolicited calls asking for personal information
  • Verify the identity of callers before sharing sensitive data
  • Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts
  • Enable two-factor authentication where available
  • Be wary of "too good to be true" offers
  • Report suspicious online activities to relevant authorities
  • Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches

Night Travel Safety

  • Plan your route in advance and stick to well-lit, populated areas
  • Inform someone of your destination and expected arrival time
  • Use reputable transportation services and verify driver details
  • Keep your phone charged and have backup power sources
  • Avoid displaying expensive items or large amounts of cash
  • Stay alert and avoid distractions like excessive phone use
  • Have emergency contacts on speed dial
  • Consider using SafeRoute's heatmap to check route safety
